In recent years, the concept of a smart home has gained significant popularity among homeowners and technology enthusiasts alike.
A smart home system is designed to connect various household appliances and devices to the internet,
allowing users to control and monitor them remotely using a smartphone, tablet, or computer.
One of the popular solutions for building a smart home system is to use a microcontroller board called Node MCU.
Node MCU is an open-source platform based on the ESP8266 Wi-Fi module that allows developers to create IoT applications easily.
By integrating Node MCU into a smart home system, users can remotely control their appliances and devices, automate tasks, and receive notifications and alerts in real-time.
This technology has brought a significant revolution in the way people live their lives, making their homes more energy-efficient, comfortable, and secure.
